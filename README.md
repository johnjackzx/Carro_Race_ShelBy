# 🏎️ Carro Race - AI-Powered Car Racing on Aptos + Shelby.xyz

**High-speed, on-chain racing game** where you mint unique AI-generated cars, race them 1v1 or in tournaments, and win APT rewards — all powered by **Aptos** blockchain with lightning-fast car visuals hosted on **Shelby.xyz**.

Carro Race demonstrates how to build performant Web3 games using:
- **Aptos Move** for on-chain logic and NFTs (using the official `aptos_token` standard)
- **Shelby.xyz** — Web3’s first cloud-grade decentralized **hot storage** protocol (co-built by Aptos Labs and Jump Crypto) for sub-second, verifiable asset delivery.

## ✨ Why This Game?

- **True ownership** — Cars are real NFTs on Aptos.
- **Fast gameplay** — Racing logic runs on-chain with Aptos randomness.
- **Blazing-fast assets** — Car renders, 3D models, and metadata load instantly from Shelby (perfect for gaming, unlike slow IPFS/Arweave cold storage).
- **AI-powered fun** — Generate unique car designs with AI and store them on Shelby for global, low-latency access.
- **Extensible** — Easy to add leaderboards, betting, multiplayer tournaments, or AI-generated tracks.

## 🎮 Game Features

- Mint unique **Car NFTs** with stats: **Speed**, **Handling**, **AI Boost**
- 1v1 races using on-chain randomness + car stats
- Winner takes a small APT reward (expandable to custom tokens or prize pools)
- Car images and metadata hosted on **Shelby.xyz** for instant loading
- Fully on-chain verifiable results
